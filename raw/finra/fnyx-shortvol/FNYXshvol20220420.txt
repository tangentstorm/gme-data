      <div class="content">
      <article class="node node--type-article node--view-mode-full clearfix node--promoted">
  
    

  <div>
      <div class="layout layout--twocol-bricks">
          <div class="row">
        <div  class="layout__region layout__region--top col-md-12">
          <div class="block-region-top"><div data-block-plugin-id="entity_field:node:title" class="block block-ctools-block block-entity-fieldnodetitle">
  
    
      <div class="content">
      <span class="field field--name-title field--type-string field--label-hidden"><h1>Short Sale Volume Data</h1></span>

    </div>
  </div>
</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    
    <div class="finra-container">
      <div class="row">
        
              </div>
    </div>

    <div class="finra-container">
      <div class="row">
        
        
              </div>
    </div>

    
      <div class="row">
        <div  class="layout__region layout__region--middle col-md-12">
          <div class="block-region-middle"><div data-block-plugin-id="entity_field:node:body" class="block block-ctools-block block-entity-fieldnodebody">
  
    
      <div class="content">
      
            <div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field__item"><p><strong style="color:#FB483D">PLEASE UPDATE YOUR BOOKMARK</strong><br /><a href="https://www.finra.org/finra-data/browse-catalog/short-sale-volume-data/daily-short-sale-volume-files"><strong>Short Sale Volume Daily Files</strong></a> moved.</p>

<p><strong>To automate access to Reg SHO data</strong>,we recommend using our <a href="https://developer.finra.org/products/equity"><strong>Equity API.</strong></a></p>

<p>

            </p><div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field__item"><div class="blue-highlight text-align-center"><strong>Please </strong><a href="https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/2C82D58"><strong>give us feedback</strong></a> <strong>on this preview of the new FINRA Data section.</strong></div>
</div>
      


<p>Pursuant to a U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission request, FINRA makes short sale trade data publicly available for off-exchange (<em>i.e.</em>, OTC) trades in exchange-listed securities reported to a FINRA <a href="/filing-reporting/trade-reporting-facility-trf">Trade Reporting Facility (TRF)</a> or the <a href="/filing-reporting/alternative-display-facililty-adf">Alternative Display Facility (ADF)</a>, as well as for trades in securities traded over-the-counter and reported to FINRAs <a href="/filing-reporting/over-the-counter-reporting-facility-orf">Over the Counter Reporting Facility (ORF)</a>. FINRA makes the data available in twoways: (1) <a href="/finra-data/short-sale-volume-data/daily-short-sale-volume-files">Daily Short Sale Volume</a>; and (2) <a href="/finra-data/short-sale-volume-data/monthly-short-sale-volume-files">Monthly Short Sale Transaction</a>(collectively, Short Sale Volume).</p>

<p>Daily Short Sale Volume provides aggregated volume by security for all short sale trades executed and reported to a TRF, the ADF, or the ORF during normal market hours for public dissemination purposes (i.e., media-reported trades). See the Daily Short Sale Volume File <a href="/sites/default/files/2021-07/DailyShortSaleVolumeFileLayout.pdf" target="_blank">formatting guide</a>.<br /><br />
The Monthly Short Sale Transaction Files provide detailed trade activity of all short sale trades executed and reported to a TRF or ADFduring normal market hours as well as after-hours. The Short Sale Files include only trades reported to a TRF or ADF for public dissemination purposes (i.e., media-reported trades).</p>

<p>As discussed more fully in <a href="/rules-guidance/notices/information-notice-051019">Information Notice 5/10/19</a>, the following key points about the Short Sale Volume may help avoid misperceptions regarding the data.</p>

<ul><li>Short Sale Volumeexcludes any trading activity that is not publicly disseminated. As a result, some offsetting buying activity related to reported short selling would not be reflected in the Daily Volumeand may result in the appearance of a higher concentration of short sale volume to total volume.</li>
	<li>The Short Sale Volume isnot consolidated with exchange data. To obtain a complete picture of the short sale volume to total volume for a particular exchange-listed stock, market participants must combine data fromeach of the TRFs and the ADF, as well as from each exchange.</li>
	<li>Short Sale Volumedoes notand isnot intended toequate to bi-monthly reported short interest position information. The short interest data reflects aggregate short positions held by market participants at a specific moment in time on two discrete days each month, while the Daily Short Sale Volume reflects the aggregate volume of trades executed and reported as short sales on each trade date.</li>
</ul><p>For additional information regarding the available data, please see <a href="/rules-guidance/notices/information-notice-051019">Information Notice 5/10/19</a> and <a href="/rules-guidance/notices/information-notice-092909">Information Notice 9/29/09</a> or contact FINRA Market Regulation at (240) 386-5126.</p>

<p><strong>The last 365 days of Short Sale Volume data is available in the grid below. See <a href="/finra-data/short-sale-volume-data/daily-short-sale-volume-files"><strong>Daily Short Sale Volume Files</strong></a> for data older than 365 days.</strong></p>
</div>
